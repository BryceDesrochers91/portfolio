Background behind how I started my YouTube channel

The reason why I started the YouTube channel was because I wanted to share my life and how I play

video games with my chin. My channel started back on December 14, 2017 and in that video I explained

what I was going to be posting to that channel. My second most viral video is "Interviewing Tyler Seguin".

The whole story behind this video is that my brother Matt decided to raise a go fund me page

so I could go to Dallas to watch a Dallas Stars hockey game and get a big behind the scenes meeting

with all the players on the team, back in January 2019. I also visited the entire American Airline Center.
